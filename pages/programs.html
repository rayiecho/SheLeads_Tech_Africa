What You Can Learn

The six tracks

TRACK 01

Web Development

12 weeks Beginner–Intermediate

Build real, deployable websites and web apps from scratch — not just tutorials you copy and forget.

  • HTML, CSS, and modern JavaScript fundamentals
  • Responsive, mobile-first design practices
  • A deployed portfolio project by week 12
TRACK 02

UI / UX Design

12 weeks Beginner-friendly

Learn to design products people actually enjoy using — from research to wireframes to polished prototypes.

  • User research and wireframing fundamentals
  • Figma, prototyping, and design systems
  • A case-study-ready portfolio project
TRACK 03

Graphic Design

10 weeks Beginner-friendly

Develop a visual design toolkit for branding, social content, and marketing materials that look professional.

  • Typography, color, and layout principles
  • Adobe / Canva workflows for brand design
  • A mini brand identity project
TRACK 04

Data Science

14 weeks Intermediate

Go from spreadsheets to real analysis — cleaning, visualizing, and drawing insight from messy data.

  • Python, Pandas, and data cleaning basics
  • Data visualization and storytelling
  • An end-to-end analysis project
TRACK 05

Cybersecurity

14 weeks Beginner–Intermediate

Understand how systems get attacked and how to defend them — foundational skills for a security career.

  • Networking and security fundamentals
  • Common threats and defensive practices
  • Hands-on labs in a safe environment
TRACK 06

Social Media Management

8 weeks Beginner-friendly

Learn to plan, create, and grow brand presence across platforms — a skill every organization needs.

  • Content strategy and calendars
  • Analytics, growth, and community management
  • A real one-month content plan you can use
